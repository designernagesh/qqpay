Our Story

How it all started?

With a humble beginning since year 2009 we operated in the heart of South East Asia located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia seeking to serve the micro business entities and migrant communities that were ignored by the banks. It was noted that banks were generally focused to serve the higher end of customer base and this directly sparked a huge mismatch and a need in the financial delivery system across many countries in South East Asia.

How We Expanded?

To form an efficient business model, we have over the years worked out strategic partnerships with reliable licensed corresponding partners located in almost more than 100 countries ranging from the Middle East, Asia, Africa, Americas and Europe continents. This has enabled us to become a global player in the money services business industry and gradually expanding our market reach over time.

Our Approach & Philosophy

We are focused to meet our business operations in line with combating money laundering activities and preventing the use of funds for illegal means. As such, staff are continuously trained to ensure such activities are hindered and it overrides any profit motive. Everyone in the company is guided by the unchanging ‘Customer First’ philosophy and implementing the “Know Your Client” rule.

By adhering to the uniqness of QQ Pay’s business model and basic principles, our Bank accounts do not earn any interest component and thus, we are not motivated to exploit customer funds to earn more income nor motivated for any delay payments.
